Fish Pempek Recipe

Description

Pempek, mpek-mpek or empek-empek is a savoury Indonesian fishcake delicacy, made of fish and tapioca, from Palembang, South Sumatera, Indonesia.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of mackerel fish meat soft grinded
  • 1 kg of sago flour
  • 250 ml of ice water
  • adequate flavouring
  • adequate salt

Steps

  1. add fish, salt, flavouring, and ice water. then mix thoroughly
  2. add flour little by little, stir until the dough becomes smooth
  3. shape the dough however you prefer. boil the pempek dough in boiling water
  4. if the dough starts to float, it means the pempek is already cooked. drain well
  5. You can serve it right away or you can fry it first
